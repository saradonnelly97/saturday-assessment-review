    Fun Facts About the Best Movie of All Time;
  1. Betty White almost turned down her role in the film because filming would require her to spend ten weeks away from her golden retriever.
  2. Despite most of the film being set in Alaska, it was actually shot in Boston and on Boston's North Shore.
  3. As Margaret grows progressively warmer throughout the film her hairstyle goes from a slick ponytail to being completely down and soft.
